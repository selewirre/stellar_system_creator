Heat DistributionÔÉÅ

Heat distribution \(\beta\) is the measure of how well a planet distributes heat, and can take values from 0 to 1. It highly depends on the rotation speed of the planet.

The faster the rotation, the closer the number is to 1 (usually 1 for all non-tidally locked planets). The heat distribution is 0.5 for a planet that is tidally locked to its parent.
