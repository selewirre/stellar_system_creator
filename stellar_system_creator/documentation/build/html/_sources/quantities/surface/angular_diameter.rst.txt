Angular Diameter
================

.. _angular_diameter:

Angular diameter :math:`\delta` is the size of a celestial body in the sky. The angular diameter of the sun and the moon are
similar on earth, and approximately ~ 0.5 degrees

The angular diameter of a body with :ref:`radius <radius>` :math:`r` at a distance :math:`D`
is given by: :math:`\delta = 2 \arcsin(r/D)`

More information on: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Angular_diameter
