Single Star Habitable Zone
==========================

.. _single_star_habitable_zone:

The Single Star Habitable Zone (SSHZ) is the simplest type of habitable zone.

For a single :ref:`star <star>` of :ref:`luminosity <luminosity>` :math:`L` and
:ref:`temperature <temperature>` :math:`T`, the habitable thresholds :math:`r_x` (where
:math:`x = {\rm inner, \, outer}`) with insolation
:math:`S_x(T)` are estimated by:
:math:`r_x = \sqrt{\frac{L}{S_x(T)}}`.
