Dense Roche Limit
=================

.. _dense_roche_limit:

Dense Roche limit is the :ref:`Roche limit <roche_limit>` of a child of unknown :ref:`radius <radius>`
and a parent object of :ref:`radius <radius>` :math:`R` :ref:`density <density>` 10 times bigger than the child :ref:`density <density>`.

It is approximately: :math:`5.2568 R`.
