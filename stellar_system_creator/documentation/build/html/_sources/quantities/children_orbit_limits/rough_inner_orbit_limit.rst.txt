Rough Inner Orbit Limit
=======================

.. rough_inner_orbit_limit:

The rough inner limit is an orbit limit given in `artifexian's video <https://youtu.be/wsuejZRqus4>`_.
For a :ref:`star <star>` similar to our sun, it is similar to the :ref:`dense Roche limit <dense_roche_limit>`
and the :ref:`rock formaiton line <outer_rock_formation_limit>`.
