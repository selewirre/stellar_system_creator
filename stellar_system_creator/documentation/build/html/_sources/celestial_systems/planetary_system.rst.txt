Planetary System
================

.. _planetary_system:

A planetary system consists of a parent/host :ref:`planet <planet>` or
a :ref:`planetary binary <binary_system>` and a number of
:ref:`satellites <satellite>` and :ref:`rings <ring>` as children.
They can also host  :ref:`trojans <trojan>` and :ref:`trojan satellites <trojan_satellite>`
on their :ref:`Lagrange positions <lagrange_position>` L4 and L5.
